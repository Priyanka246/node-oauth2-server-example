Secondary information about the service

Patients

Get access to all of your NHS health data, manage and track your health with supportive resources and share your needs and progress with your clinical teams and carers.

Carers

Get access to all the tools that a patient has including a carer dashboard to manage the health and wellbeing of those closest to you on their behalf.

Clinical teams

Monitor your caseload and interact with patients and carers. Send resources, messages, questionnaires, appointments and more.

Admins

Manage clinical teams, create resource packs, schedule notifications and manage global settings.
